Find the right school for your child.

Welcome to the City of Chicago's Early Learning Portal. Here you can easily find early learning programs near your home or work. You can also compare programs side-by-side to select the highest quality option for your child. Browse the FAQ page to learn more about different alternatives and how to enroll your child in a quality early learning program.

Searchor Just start browsing

Find early learning centers near you.

Search by the address where you live or work to quickly find all of the early learning centers located near you. The results are shown in a list and on a map so you can find what you want the way you find it easiest.

Browse locations by neighborhood.

You can pick a neighborhood from the list or map if you don't want to search by address. You can also type a neighborhood or even a school name into the search bar to get there faster.

Get detailed information about each location.

Each location has details about the types of programs they run, contact info, additional languages, and more! Star schools you like to come back later, or share them with friends and family.

Refine your search to find exactly what you want.

There are a number of ways to narrow down your search so you can see only the programs and schools you are interested in.
