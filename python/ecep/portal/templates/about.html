About the Chicago Early Learning Portal

The Chicago Early Learning Portal is a collaboration between the City of Chicago, the Chicago Public Schools, the Chicago Department of Family and Support Services, the Smart Chicago Collaborative, and the University of Chicago Urban Education Lab.

Mayor Rahm Emanuel has made investment in early childhood education a cornerstone of his administration, with the goal of serving at-risk children and increasing the transparency and accountability of city programs.

Announcing this initiative in September 2011, the Mayor remarked: “We must provide children in every neighborhood with the tools they need to excel in school and succeed in the workforce, and that includes providing excellent early childhood education . . . these reforms will help parents find opportunities for their children and better prepare our students for the rigors of elementary, middle and high school classrooms.”

For technical assistance or for more information about the Early Learning Portal, please contact info@chicagoearlylearning.org.

For general questions about early learning options in Chicago, including information about enrollment, eligibility, and payment, please refer to our FAQ page.

Partners and Sponsors

City of Chicago

Chicago Public Schools

Chicago Public Schools works to ensure that every student in every community has access to a high quality education that prepares them for success in college and career.
Chicago Department of Family and Support Services

The Chicago Department of Family & Support Services (DFSS) is dedicated to supporting a continuum of coordinated services to enhance the lives of Chicago residents, particularly those most in need, from birth through the senior years.
Smart Chicago Collaborative

The Smart Chicago Collaborative is a civic organization devoted to using technology to make lives better in Chicago. We work to apply the transformative power of technology to solve problems for the people of Chicago.
University of Chicago Urban Education Lab

The University of Chicago Urban Education Lab, a project of the Urban Education Institute, seeks to improve education outcomes by providing scientific evidence about what strategies are the most effective and cost effective, for whom, and why. [link to: http://uel.uchicago.edu/]
