Enrolling in Chicago: Ready to Learn! early learning programs is easy.

First, find an enrollment site near you.

For community-based programs, enroll directly at your preferred provider's location. Navigate the Chicago Early Learning Portal to identify a provider that will suit your needs, or call the Chicago: Ready to Learn! hotline at (312) 229-1690 for one-on-one assistance.

For CPS school-based programs, apply at any of the following child-friendly locations:

  • Loop, 42 W. Madison Street, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • Colman, 4655 S. Dearborn Street, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • Hall Mall, 4638 W. Diversey Avenue, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Visit cps.edu/readytolearn to learn more about school based enrollment, including additional enrollment opportunities and what to bring.

Call the Chicago: Ready to Learn! Hotline at (312) 229-1690 for one-on-one assistance.

Next, visit your program's site to begin the process of enrolling your child!

A specialist will be there to walk you through the process step-by-step. Many people find it helpful to make a plan to visit an enrollment site.

That’s it! Ready to get started?

Join nearly 40,000 Chicago children participating in early learning programs. Classes are starting soon…

1) Give us a call or a visit;
2) Discuss options with a specialist;
3) Enroll your child for preschool today!
