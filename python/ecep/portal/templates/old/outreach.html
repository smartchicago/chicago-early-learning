Outreach 2017

Thank you for helping spread the word about enrolling 3 and 4 year olds in early learning programs across Chicago. With your help, we will enroll more children than ever before, especially our highest need families.

Here you will find social media links, talking points, and outreach resources to help share information with Chicago families.

Tell

Tell families about Chicago Early Learning. Here are some talking points you can share with your families:

The Chicago Early Learning online preschool application is now open! Follow these easy steps to get your child enrolled in preschool:

Step 1) Visit ChicagoEarlyLearning.org to create an online account for your family

Step 2) Fill in information on your family, such as employment and income, information on your child, and then apply for your top choice preschool site

Step 3) If your child qualifies, you will receive an email on June 1st with information on how to verify your child's preschool seat

See you for the first day of school in September!

If you have questions, please visit ChicagoEarlyLearning.org, or call the Chicago Early Learning Hotline: (312) 229-1690.

There are also great resources on ChicagoEarlyLearning.org:

  • Visit the FAQs page to get answers to common questions
  • Tell parents about the beneifts of early learning

  • With over 600 options throughout the city let us help you find the best match for your 3 and 4 year old. Get started at chicagoearlylearning.org #chicagoearlylearning
